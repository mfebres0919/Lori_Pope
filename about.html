About

L.A. Pope

Hi there, I’m Lori Ann. Most people call me L.A.—either name is great.

I got my start behind the mic as a radio DJ in Providence and Boston, and a few years ago I found my way back to voiceover in an unexpected way while working in experiential marketing at Facebook. What began as a one-off opportunity turned into ongoing VO work for VOG announcements at global events, as well as commercial spots and narration for case studies.

That background means I don’t just deliver lines, I understand brand voice and how to bring it to life with the right tone, pace, and presence.

When a project calls for broadcast-quality audio and finishing, I’m proud to partner with David Hughes at Shine On Studio Oakland. I also record and edit from my home studio for fast, reliable turnaround.
